About us

SakaHapa s a sourcing App where a buyer can specify the kind of product, he/she is looking to purchase and any seller within a 40km radius that has the same item or something similar to it can respond with a picture and price. We only point buyers in the direction of the most affordable seller for the item they desire. This saves them time they would have otherwise spent physically sourcing for the specific product they want. Currently we have incorporated the following categories into the App: Fashion, Electronics, Home, furniture & appliances, Mobile phone & tablet, Vehicle & vehicle parts, Health & beaty and Property.



Buyer Interface

We provide the buyer with a specialized search feature accessed by clicking on the '+' button at the bottom of their Home page. Here, they can post a sample image of their product if they have one, describe the item in terms of color, material, size, et cetera and choose the correct category in order to post their request.

I. Buyer's home page

Any seller with the same item or something similar to it will respond to the buyer's request with a picture and price of their product. The buyer will then view all the seller responses of a particular post. They can either accept a product if it matches their taste or decline if it doesn't.

II. Single seller's respomse

Once a buyer accepts a product, they have to make initial contact with the merchant since sellers have no access to buyers contact information. The buyer can decide to visit the vendor's premises (If they love to inspect items before purchasing), plan a meet-up at a convenient pick-up location or arrange for delivery at an extra cost to be facilitated by the buyer.



Seller Interface

Sellers on the other hand view all buyer requests on their landing page. If they are able to meet a particular buyer's specifications after going through them, they can respond to the post by clicking on the 'Respond' button inside the specific post

III. New seller response

Once a buyer receives a seller response, they are at liberty to accept or reject the product depending on how close it matches their uploaded specifications. The seller will then receive feedback of the buyer's response as either accepted for a welcomed response, rejected for a declined response or pending for a response that has not received any buyer feedback.
