About us

SakaHapa  is a Mobile Application that connects buyers with sellers who deal in fashion products & accessories within a 40km geographical radius. Through our platform a buyer can specify what he/she is looking to purchase and any seller that has the same item or something similar to it can respond with a picture and price of their product.

Once the customer identifies what they are interested in they can decide to visit the merchant’s premises and purchase directly from the seller or contact them and arrange for delivery at an extra cost. Payment is done once the shopper receives and inspects the quality of their merchandise. If a client is not able to find what they are looking for from our list of available products, they can use our specialized search feature on the buyer's "MY ORDERS" page where they can post a request with the description of what they want and any seller that has the same item or something similar to it will respond with their offer.

Seller Interface


Upon registration, the seller will be given 30 free days to test the app. After expiry of the free trial, they will be required to make a monthly subscription of Ksh.300 per month to continue using the app. Once they are enrolled on the app, they will be allocated 10 slots to advertise their products for 30 days. This means that they can only post 10 products at a time on the buyer explore page and after 30 days those products will expire and will no longer be available.

IV. Uploaded seller products

However, this does not mean that until the 30 days elapse the seller cannot upload a new product. They can choose to delete an item and replace it with another as long as their profile does not exceed 10 products at any given time. Another additional feature for the seller is the ability to get statistics on the 10 products they have uploaded. From their dashboard, they are able to see how many buyers have viewed a certain product and how many have added it to their collection to purchase in the future. For example: Seller XYZ has uploaded a pair of high heels that go for Ksh.4000. On their end, the seller will be able to see on that specific product how many people viewed it and how many proceeded to add to collection. These statistics help the seller to make informed choices such as when to delete a product that does not have favorable statistics or re- upload the same product if it is doing well.

Buyer Interface


On the other hand, buyers are not required to make any form of subscription. They browse through the uploaded products and if they see a product they are interested in they can contact the seller and arrange to purchase it. They also have access to a filter function where they can select products based on price and distance. Another special feature for the buyers is the ability to make a special request if whatever they are looking for has not been uploaded by any of the sellers. For example, buyer ABC can post a request that they are looking for a slim fit, blue, faded jeans, waist 28 and also upload a sample image to make their request more specific. For this specialized service the buyer will be charged only Ksh.20 per request and all sellers within their vicinity (40km radius) will be able to view what they intend to purchase. Any seller that has something close to the requested item will respond with a photo of their product & price. For this personalized advertising to a willing buyer the seller will be charged Ksh.5 to respond to a buyer’s post.

VI. Seller purchase responses

The buyer will then review and accept or reject the offers via the app while the sellers will receive notification on the status of their products (whether it was accepted or rejected).
