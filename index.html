About us

SakaHapa  is a Mobile Application that connects buyers with sellers who deal in fashion products & accessories within a 40km geographical radius. Through our platform a buyer can specify what he/she is looking to purchase and any seller that has the same item or something similar to it can respond with a picture and price of their product.



Buyer Interface

We provide the buyer with a specialized search feature accessed by clicking on the ‘+ New’ button at the bottom of their Home page. Here, they can post a sample image of their product if they have one, describe the item in terms of color, material, size, et cetera and choose the correct category in order to post their request.

I. Buyer's home page

Any seller with the same item or something similar to it will respond to the buyer’s request with a picture and price of their product. The buyer will be required to pay ksh.20 only to view all the seller responses of a particular post. They can either accept the product if it matches their taste or decline if it doesn’t

II. Pay to view your responses

II. Single seller's respomse

Once a buyer accepts a product they have to make initial contact with the merchant since sellers have no access to buyers contact information. The buyer can decide to visit the vendor’s premises (If they love to try outfits before purchasing), plan a meet-up at a convenient pick-up location or arrange for delivery at an extra cost to be facilitated by the buyer.



Seller Interface

Sellers on the other hand view all buyer requests on their landing page. If they are able to meet a particular buyer’s specifications after going through them they can respond to the post by clicking on the ‘+ Respond’ button inside the specific post.

III. New seller response

One seller response goes for an average of Ksh.5. This table below shows how responses are priced.



Responses Price
2 responses Ksh.10
4 responses ksh.20
10 responses Ksh.50
22 responses ksh.100
110 responses Ksh.500

Once a buyer receives a seller response they are at liberty to accept or reject the product depending on how close it matches their uploaded specifications. The seller will then receive feedback of the buyer’s response as either accepted for an accepted response, rejected for a rejected response or pending for a response that has not received a buyer feedback.
